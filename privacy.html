Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FrameReady Camera, whose iPhone icon is shown as FrameReady, handles information in connection with the iOS app, support communication and these GitHub Pages legal pages.

Short version: FrameReady Camera is designed as a local-first creator camera app. Camera preview, recordings, thumbnails, cover frames, scripts, project data and settings are intended to stay on your device unless you choose to export, share, save to Photos or contact support.

Scope

This policy covers the FrameReady Camera iOS app, support emails and the legal pages hosted for the app. Apple processes App Store downloads, subscriptions, in-app purchases, refunds and related account data under Apple’s own terms and privacy framework.

Camera, face and biometric note

FrameReady Camera may show local framing assistance around a face or relevant content area. In the current version, this is intended only for geometric placement support. It is not designed for biometric identification, face recognition, identity verification, surveillance, emotional assessment, beauty assessment, health assessment or profiling.

What we do not use in the current version

  • No user account required.
  • No provider-operated cloud sync.
  • No provider server upload of private video clips, scripts, thumbnails or cover frames.
  • No third-party analytics SDK.
  • No crash reporting SDK.
  • No advertising tracking.
  • No sale of personal data.

Legal bases under GDPR

  • Contract performance or pre-contract steps for app functionality, Premium access and support, Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
  • Legal obligations where records must be kept, Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR.
  • Legitimate interests for website security, support handling, abuse prevention and reliable operation, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.
  • Consent where a feature or system permission explicitly asks for it, Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.

iOS permissions

The app may request access to camera, microphone and Photos depending on the feature you use. You can change permissions in iOS Settings. If you deny a permission, the related feature may not work.

Storage and deletion

Local app data remains on your device unless you delete it, delete the app, use an in-app delete/reset option where available, or share/export it yourself. Videos and images saved to Apple Photos must be managed in the Photos app. Support emails are kept only as long as needed to answer and document the request, subject to legal retention duties.

Children

The app is intended as a creator productivity tool. It is not specifically directed at children. The app does not create user accounts or knowingly collect children’s personal data for provider-operated services. Parents or guardians should supervise recording and publishing decisions for minors.

Processors and third parties

  • Apple: App Store downloads, in-app purchases, subscriptions, refunds, entitlement infrastructure and Photos/iOS system services.
  • Email provider: Support communication if you contact us.
  • GitHub Pages: Hosting of these legal pages.

These providers may process data under their own terms and may use infrastructure outside your country. Where legally required, appropriate safeguards should apply.

App Store privacy information

The App Store privacy label and this policy must match the actual app build. If future versions add analytics, cloud sync, crash reporting, accounts, advertising, external AI services or other processing, this policy and App Store privacy information must be updated before release.
