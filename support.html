Support

Need help with FrameReady Camera, whose iPhone icon is shown as FrameReady, subscriptions or a technical issue? Contact support by email.

F.Kintzel@gmail.com

Please include

  • Your device model.
  • Your iOS version.
  • The app version/build if visible.
  • A short description of the issue.
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if possible.
  • Whether the issue concerns recording, projects, scripts, cover selection, export, paywall or subscription restore.

Subscriptions

Purchases, subscriptions, cancellations, refunds and billing are handled by Apple. For billing issues, Apple Support or your Apple account subscription settings may be the right place to start.

Privacy-friendly support

Please do not send unnecessary private videos, faces, names, addresses, screenshots with sensitive content or identifying information about other people. A short technical description is usually enough.

Not for emergencies

Support email is not monitored as an emergency channel. FrameReady Camera is a creator productivity app and is not an emergency, safety, medical, legal or crisis service.

Deutsch, Support kurz gefasst

Bei technischen Problemen sende bitte eine kurze Beschreibung, dein Gerät, deine iOS Version, die App Version und die Schritte zur Reproduktion. Bitte sende keine unnötigen privaten Videos oder sensiblen Inhalte. Käufe, Abos, Kündigungen und Erstattungen laufen über Apple.
