Privacy-first setup

The current app design does not use user accounts, provider-operated cloud sync, third-party analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs or advertising tracking. Recordings, scripts, thumbnails, cover frames and project data are intended to stay on your device unless you export, share or contact support.
