Privacy-first setup

The current version is designed without user accounts, provider-operated cloud sync, third-party analytics SDK, crash reporting SDK or advertising tracking. Recordings, scripts, thumbnails, cover frames and project data are intended to stay on your device unless you decide to export, share or contact support.
